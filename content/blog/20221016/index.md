**Data Modeling Guide**

> Microservices or monolith. SPA or classic. Ruby or typed languages. I have opinions on all of it and they are “few applications sufficiently invests in data modeling and database design.” Do that first

> Learn how data modeling can be a key enabler of #microservices for transactional and analytics systems

> How do microservices punt on data modeling? They break down domains too much making it really hard to see the big picture

> Data Modelling is only relevant in context, there is no modelling without context.  The biggest issue with traditional data folks is they think there is a contextless model.

> My favorite is when schemaless db’s will be like “our db follows strict JSON schema, providing flexible data modeling to the developer.” Please. U know good and wel that 95% of folks are gonna be messy and not use any design.
